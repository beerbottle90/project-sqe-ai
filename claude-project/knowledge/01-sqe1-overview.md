# SQE1 — Overview, Format and Syllabus

*Reference note for the SQE1 Study Coach project. Figures stated as at June 2026;
always confirm dates and fees on the SRA website before relying on them.*

## What SQE1 is

SQE1 is Part 1 of the Solicitors Qualifying Examination, the route to qualifying
as a **solicitor of England and Wales**. It tests **Functioning Legal Knowledge
(FLK)** — your ability to apply the law, not merely recite it.

## Structure

- Two assessments: **FLK1** and **FLK2**.
- Each assessment has **180 single best answer multiple choice questions**
  → **360 questions in total**.
- Each question has a scenario, a question stem, and **five options (A–E)**;
  there is exactly **one best answer**.
- Each assessment is sat in **two sessions of 90 questions** over consecutive
  half-days.
- There is **no negative marking**; one mark per correct answer.
- The **pass mark is set per sitting** by the SRA (it is not a fixed percentage).

## Subjects

**FLK1**
- Business Law and Practice
- Dispute Resolution
- Contract
- Tort
- Legal System of England and Wales
- Constitutional and Administrative Law and EU Law
- Legal Services

**FLK2**
- Property Practice
- Wills and the Administration of Estates
- Solicitors Accounts
- Land Law
- Trusts
- Criminal Liability and Criminal Law and Practice

**Ethics and Professional Conduct** is **not a separate section** — it is examined
**pervasively** across both FLK1 and FLK2. Treat it as always potentially in play.

## Approximate weighting

The SRA sets a question-count **range** per topic rather than a fixed number, so
the split varies slightly between sittings. As a planning guide:
- Each substantive subject: roughly **14–20%** of its paper (~25–36 questions).
- Legal Services: roughly **12–16%** (~22–29 questions).

## Sittings, dates and fees (as at June 2026)

- SQE1 is offered **twice a year** — a **January** sitting and a **July** sitting,
  at Pearson VUE test centres worldwide.
- **July 2026 SQE1:** assessment window **13–24 July 2026**; results expected
  **~8 September 2026**; registration **closed on 28 May 2026**.
- **Fee:** **£1,934** for both FLK papers (rising to **£2,006** from
  September 2026). A single-paper resit is roughly half. Fees are VAT-exempt.
- Booking is first-come, first-served — book early.

## Qualified-lawyer route (relevant to a Turkish-qualified lawyer)

- **SQE2 exemption** is the realistic concession for an experienced foreign
  lawyer: you show, through qualifications and professional legal work
  experience, that you already have the skills SQE2 assesses (commonly with
  ~2+ years' experience). This usually leaves **SQE1 as the assessment to sit**.
- **SQE1 exemption** is very hard to obtain; the SRA itself suggests sitting the
  assessment instead.
- Still required regardless of exemptions: **character and suitability**,
  **English (or Welsh) language** competence, and **two years of Qualifying Work
  Experience** (prior practice can count).
- Apply via **mySRA** → "Apply for qualified lawyer exemption from the SQE
  assessments".
